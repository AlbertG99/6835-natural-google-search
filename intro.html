Welcome to Natural Google Search!

You can use this application to more naturally search for and browse results on Google.

The red dot acts as your cursor. To move it, move your hand over the Leap Motion sensor in front of you. To scroll, move the red dot towards the corresponding edge of the window.

You can also use voice commands to perform various actions:

  1. Navigation:
    • search for QUERY: start a new Google search for QUERY
    • click or open or select: open the link at the red dot
    • back or return: return to the previous page
  2. Scrolling:
    • reverse: reverse the vertical scrolling direction
    • upwards or down: scroll a bit up or down, respectively
  3. Pausing: if you need a break, or just want more time to read the page
    • pause: temporarily disable all voice commands and scrolling
    • unpause or resume (only enabled commands): resume all voice commands and scrolling
  4. Other:
    • instructions: see these instructions again at any time

You will see your recognized voice input in the top left corner of your screen.

Before proceeding, you can practice scrolling up and down on this page.

When you are ready to get started, use the Leap Motion sensor and your voice to click here!
Or use the search for voice command to start a search!
