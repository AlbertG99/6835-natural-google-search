Welcome to Natural Google Search!

You can use this application to more naturally search for and browse results on Google.

The red dot acts as your cursor. To move it, move your hand over the Leap Motion sensor in front of you. To scroll, move the red dot towards the corresponding edge of the window.

You can also use voice commands to perform various actions:

  • search for QUERY: start a new Google search for QUERY
  • click: click the element at the red dot
  • back or return: return to the previous page
  • upwards or down: scroll a bit up or down, respectively
When you are ready to get started, use the Leap Motion sensor and your voice to click here! Or use the search for voice command to start a search!
